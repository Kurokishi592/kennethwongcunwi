VEX Tower Takeover (2020)

VEX V5 • Hwa Chong Infocomm & Robotics Society • May 2019 – Jan 2020

Overview

Developed a VEX robot and competed in a 2v2 points-based game. Applied mechanism design experience (lifts, linkages, flywheels, pneumatics) and iteration under competition constraints.
