## Overview

A fast-paced embedded IoT build: detect device tilt and high infra-red (IR) light and trigger immediate visual and audible alarms. The device can also be monitored and controlled remotely via a web UI.

## Highlights

- FreeRTOS firmware with ISRs, semaphores, queues, and task prioritization.
- Dual-MCU architecture (FRDM + ESP32).
- Sensors: tilt switch, IR sensor, IMU.
