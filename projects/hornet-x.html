Hornet X

Bumblebee Autonomous Systems training • Sep 2024 – Mar 2025

What I built

  • Designed and soldered a custom sensors daughterboard PCB integrating LIS3MDL magnetometer, MPU6500 accel/gyro, and Teensy 4.1.
  • Wrote a custom sensor fusion pipeline using polar coordinates and quaternions, then applied a Kalman filter for robust yaw/roll/pitch.
  • Worked with I2C, SPI, and CAN bus interfacing and general electrical debugging.
